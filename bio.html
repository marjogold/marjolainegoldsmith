Marjolaine Goldsmith is an actress born, raised and based in New York.

She graduated from Oberlin College with a degree in Classical Civilization.

Now, she is a proud member of Teater of War, a social impact company that utilizes the Classics to spark positive discussions and foster community and change.

She currentyly studies acting with Wynn Handman.

Her dream roles include but are not limitied to; Amy Winehouse and Franny Glass
