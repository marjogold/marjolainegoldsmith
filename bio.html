--- layout: default title: Bio | Marjolaine Goldsmith ---

Marjolaine Goldsmith is an actress born, raised and based in New York.

She graduated from Oberlin College with a degree in Classical Civilization.

Now, she is a proud member of Theater of War, a social impact company that utilizes the Classics to spark positive discussions, foster community, and promote change. For more information about her work with Theater of War Productions check out this article.

She currently studies acting with Wynn Handman.

Her dream roles include, but are not limitied to; Amy Winehouse and Salinger's Franny Glass. Her Dream TV show to work on would be a love child of Twin Peaks, Curb Your Enthusiasm, and THat 70's Show.
